Student Visa

Students aspiring to study in foreign destinations to expand their career horizons need a student visa to pursue their dreams. This form of visa requires an enormous amount of documentation and has a range of procedures that need to be followed. This can be a long and tiresome process at times. At One Stop Solutions we aim to make this process smooth and hassle free for all our clients.

Work Permit

Talent and skilled individuals are always in demand in every corner of the world. Work permit visa is essential to enable an individual to work in a foreign destination.
The process starts by validation of the job offer in favor of the concerned individual and is followed by other complex stages. The stages in this process vary depending on the country and usually has a strict deadline attached to it.

Tourist Visa

A tourist visa is usually a short term visa required when visiting a country for the purpose of pleasure. The purpose of the visit needs to be made clear and the individual also needs to show clear intent of returning to the country of origin once the purpose is served. The applicant also needs to specify the exact amount of time that they are going to spend in the country.

Business Visa

Traveling abraod is a great way to take your business to new heights and places around the world. A business visa enables an individual to visit a country for the purpose of conducting business like company meetings, conferences and conventions. Essential documents like the letter of invitation and additional information about the visit is vital to the process.

Permanent Residency

Permanent residency grants the individual the status of a permanent resident of the country, making them eligible for all benefits enjoyed by natural born citizens. Most english speaking countries require a proof of english proficiency before they grant a PR visa, which can be provided by taking various exams like IELTS and TOEFL.

Dependent Visa

This Visa permits the legal resident's immediate family to travel to a foreign country for the purpose of accompanying the family member with a corresponding Visa. The proof of relationship is the main step in this process which needs to be presented to move forward along with other documents.
